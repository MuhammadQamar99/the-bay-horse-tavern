Our Story

A Manchester Institution Since 1985

Nestled in the vibrant streets of Manchester, The Bay Horse Tavern has been serving exceptional food and drink for over three decades. What began as a humble pub has evolved into one of the city's most beloved dining destinations.

Our commitment to quality ingredients, traditional recipes with contemporary flair, and warm hospitality has earned us a loyal following among locals and visitors alike.
